About Us

Built on
the Court

Mana Basketball is a premier training organization in California dedicated to developing the next generation of basketball talent. Our philosophy centers on building complete players — combining elite skill work with mental toughness and game IQ.

Whether you're a young athlete taking your first steps on the court or an experienced player pushing toward the next level, our coaching staff brings decades of competitive experience to every session. We don't just train players — we build competitors.

Programs

01

Youth Development

Building foundations that last a lifetime. Our youth program focuses on fundamentals, coordination, and a genuine love for the game.

  • Ages 8–14
  • Fundamentals & footwork
  • Small group sessions
  • Weekend clinics available
02

Elite Training

For committed athletes ready to compete at the highest levels. Advanced skill development, film study, and performance conditioning.

  • Ages 14–18
  • Advanced ball handling & shooting
  • Game film analysis
  • College prep & exposure
03

Team Camps

Intensive team-focused training camps designed to build chemistry, sharpen strategy, and prepare squads for competitive play.

  • Full teams & AAU squads
  • Offensive & defensive systems
  • Scrimmage & competition
  • Custom camp packages
